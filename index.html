I AM GROOT

Groot (/ɡruːt/) is a character appearing in American comic books published by Marvel Comics. Created by Stan Lee, Larry Lieber and Jack Kirby, the character first appeared in Tales to Astonish #13 (November 1960).

THE DEADPOOL
